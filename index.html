How to play

Welcome to the Barista Bar

You're behind the counter for a 5-customer shift. Each customer asks for a drink with a flavor cue — your job is to read the order and pour it right.

  1. 1Pick the beans. Match the customer's flavor cue (fruity, nutty, earthy…) to one of four origins.
  2. 2Build the drink. Use +/− to add parts of espresso, milk, foam, water, or syrup. Watch the cup fill — get the ratio right.
  3. 3Serve. Score up to 100 barista points per drink based on bean match and ratio accuracy.

Wren, the head barista, will drop tips along the way. New to coffee? Read the history & guide first.
